I made this little page because you deserve more than a quick message.

You’re the one I call when I get really down, and I hate that in this moment I wasn’t that person for you.

I’m really sorry, baby. For real. Not the fake sorry that people throw around just to move past it this one sits deep. I should’ve hit you up some other way even if I thought you were reflecting. School stress plus no phone plus your girl not reaching out? Tho you said you were fine......That’s a bad combo, and I added to it without knowing. I own that.

You mean a lot to me, baby. We’ve built something solid the kind of friendship where we can abuse each other one minute and have each other’s backs the next. I don’t take that for granted. I’ve been replaying that day in my head, wishing I could rewind and just walk up like normal. “Wassup baby, are you cool?" Simple as that. But I can’t change it, so I’m here now trying to fix it the best way I know how by being honest and telling you I see how my action hurt.(Tho ion know how much you're hurt )

I value you more than that, and I want to show it better going forward.

Can we scratch this? I’m ready to make it right however you need. Just know I’m here, and I’m not going anywhere. You’re my home boy for real, and I got you moving forward. No more assumptions on my end. I’ll check on you even if it seems like you’re just reflecting, because that’s what we normally do.
